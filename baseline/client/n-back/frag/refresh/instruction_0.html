For the zero-back task, you will press the space bar when the current number on the screen is the number "1".

For example, if you saw "6, 1, 7", you would press the space bar when the 1 is on the screen.
You will see this image before the zero-back starts:
Zero-Back
6 1 7
When you see this image, you will press the number “0” in order to start the zero-back task. Once the zero-back task has started, you will always press the space bar for the number 1 during the zero-back task.

Please press the space bar to continue.
