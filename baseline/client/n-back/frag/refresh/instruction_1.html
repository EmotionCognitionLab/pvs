For the one-back task, you will press the space bar when the current number on the screen is the same as the number shown immediately before the current number.

For example, if you saw "5, 7, 7", you would press the space bar when the second 7 is on the screen.
You will see this image before the one-back starts:
One-Back
5 7 7
You will always press the space bar for the target number. The target number can be any number between 1-9.

Press the space bar to continue.
