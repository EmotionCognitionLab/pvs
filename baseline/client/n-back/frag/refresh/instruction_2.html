For the two-back task, you will press the spacebar when the current number on the screen is the same as the number shown two positions back.

For example, if you saw "6, 3, 6", you would press the spacebar when the second 6 is on the screen.
You will see this image before the two-back starts:
Two-Back
6 3 6
You will always press the spacebar for the target number. The target number can be any number between 1-9.

Press the space bar to continue.
